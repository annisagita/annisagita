SIPENCATAR 2021 | SCHOOLPAD

Student admission system 2021 for Ministry of Transportation





To assist the implementation of the online ministry of transportation student admissions which has been running for more than 5 years, the 2021 sipencatar application was attended by 41,000+ Participants from all over Indonesia. This application is a means for students candidate to carry out each stage of the exam and also helps the admissions team work.


Website : sipencatar.dephub.go.id

My role :

For this project I managed a team to meet client needs (the ministry of transportation) by using agile methodology, using jira for task management, using mattermost and whatsapp group to communicate, and for meetings we used google meet and zoom alternately. I also do testing for applications and communicate with clients to ensure the system is in accordance with client needs. In addition to running from the management side of software development, I also made guidebooks and technical meetings for each part of the team so that this test ran smoothly.
